About Our Church

Growing Together in Faith

Grace Community Church has been serving the local community for over 30 years. We are a diverse congregation committed to following Jesus Christ and sharing His love with everyone we meet.

Our mission is to make disciples who love God, love people, and serve the world. We believe in the power of community and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

Whether you're new to faith or have been a Christian for years, you'll find a welcoming community where you can grow spiritually and connect with others on the same journey.
